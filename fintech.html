My Journey in Machine Learning at a Fusion Startup

At a pioneering fusion startup, I embarked on an ambitious project to develop a stock price prediction tool, integrating machine learning with complex financial data. My challenge was to create a model that not only predicted but also understood the intricate dynamics of the stock market.

Collaborating with a team of experts, I focused on a novel approach: utilizing a greedy algorithm that explored temporal and phase derivatives as its search space. This journey led to groundbreaking insights and models that could adapt and learn from ever-evolving market data.

Greedy Algorithm: Harnessing Temporal and Phase Derivatives

In this project, the greedy algorithm was designed to iteratively select and combine temporal and phase derivatives, constructing a predictive model uniquely suited for financial data analysis. By exploring this rich search space, the model could capture subtle patterns and changes over time and between different market indicators.

Highlights of this project included:

  • Development of a sophisticated algorithm to analyze time-dependent and spatial relationships in financial data.
  • Innovative use of high-order temporal and phase derivatives to enhance prediction accuracy.
  • Dynamic adaptation of the model to reflect real-time market changes and trends.
