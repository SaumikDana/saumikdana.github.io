â€¢ Right child: all data points where X â‰¥ 5
**Recursively repeat the process**

â€¢ For the right child, find the best split among all its data points
â€¢ Each child becomes a new decision node with its own best split
**Continue until stopping criteria**

â€¢ Maximum depth reached
â€¢ Minimum samples per leaf reached
â€¢ Information gain below threshold
â€¢ Node becomes "pure enough"
**Create leaf nodes**
When splitting stops, the node becomes a leaf that predicts the **mean** of all Y values in that node
