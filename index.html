Strategic Earnings Forecast: A Five-Factor Analysis for Informed Stock Selection

Strategic framework for selecting stocks before earnings releases, focusing on options volume, put/call ratio, implied volatility, historical price movements, and financial health. High options volume may signal investor interest, while a favorable put/call ratio indicates bullish sentiment. Elevated implied volatility often forecasts significant price shifts, making it a critical pre-earnings indicator. Analyzing a stock’s historical response to earnings can predict its potential movement. Lastly, robust financial health is crucial to ensure the company has a strong foundation regardless of short-term market reactions to earnings. Read more
